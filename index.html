Banaras Hindu University

Situated in Varanasi in Uttar Pradesh, one of the oldest cities. BHU was founded in 1916 and offers over 300 courses across 20 streams which includes Arts, Science, Law, Management, Agriculture, Banking, IT, Engineering.

Home to 12000 students

The campus is in semicircular form and the design of the building is an example of Indo-Gothic architecture. It has more than 60 hostels and has been a home to more than twelve thousand students.
